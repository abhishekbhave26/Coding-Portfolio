Dr Vijay Bhave alias B Prayog is the proprietor of B Prayog Infotainment.
He is a doctor by profession and has been practising for the last 27 years.
He has had his music albums released by T Series and recently by Red Ribbon Entertainment.
B Prayog is a IPRS certified music director and composer.
His songs have been sung by the likes of Shankar Mahadevan,Sudhesh Bhosale,Sadhana Sargam among many others.
